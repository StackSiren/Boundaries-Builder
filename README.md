# 🧱 Boundaries Builder

**Boundaries Builder** is a simple, visual tool for defining and organizing healthy personal boundaries. Using drag‑and‑drop blocks, you can create a clear boundary map for reflection, growth, and communication.

***

## ✨ Features

*   Drag‑and‑drop boundary blocks  
    ⏰ Time · 📍 Space · 💬 Communication · ⚡ Energy · ❤️ Emotional
*   Visual boundary canvas
*   Save & load layouts
*   Export as PDF or image
*   Calm, minimal design

***

## 🎯 Why Boundaries Builder?

*   Clarifies personal limits
*   Supports healing and self‑reflection
*   Makes boundaries easier to express
*   Encourages self‑respect and balance

***

## 🚀 How to Use

1.  Drag boundary blocks into the canvas
2.  Arrange them to match your needs
3.  Save or export your boundaries

There is no “right” layout—only what works for you.

***

## 🤍 Philosophy

Boundaries are not walls; they are acts of self‑care.  
**You are worthy of healthy boundaries.**
